Tools
	Silicon Labs - Wireless Development Suite (WDS3)
		- https://www.silabs.com/documents/login/software/WDS3-Setup.exe

	ZakKemble Radio Config (ZRC)
		- https://github.com/ZakKemble/Si446x
		- radio_config.exe or radio_config.pl (Perl script).
		- Converts radio_config_Si4463.h to radio_config.h.
Procedure
	- With WDS, open/create configuration.xml.
	- Generate radio_config_Si4463.h file.
	- With ZRC, convert radio_config_Si4463.h to radio_config.h. On Windows, drag file to exe.

Channel Hopping is mandatory, to conform to duty cycle restrictions.

Configurations:

- LoLa868Config
	- 99 channels. 
	- [865.05;  866.95] MHz, 868MHz Europe ISM band with 50KHz margin.
	- 250kbps baudrate.

- LoLa433Config
	- 35 channels. 
	- [433.05 ;  434.775] MHz, 433MHz Europe ISM band with 25KHz margin.
	- 250kbps baudrate.
